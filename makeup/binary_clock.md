# Binary Clock 

Targeting 60 - 90 mins

## Goals

* Bring together lots of different tools into one awesome-sauce thing


## Materials

* Raspberry Pi
* Mount
* Bread Board
* Ribbon Cable
* 5 Red LEDs
* 5 Green LEDs
* 3 Blue LEDs
* Long HDMI cable
* Speaker for the Angelus

* Maybe even split into 2 groups?
